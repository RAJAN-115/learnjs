Ladli Bahen Yojana e-KYC Guide

1. Access the Official Portal

  1. Open your browser and go to: https://cmladlibahna.mp.gov.in
  2. Click the e-KYC banner on the homepage.

2. Enter Aadhaar & Captcha

  1. Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
  2. Type the on-screen captcha code.

3. OTP Verification

  1. Click Send OTP.
  2. Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.
  3. Click Submit to complete verification.

4. Confirm Personal Details

  1. Review your Name, Address, and Samagra ID.
  2. Click Continue or Proceed if all details are correct.

5. Upload Required Documents

If prompted, upload clear scans or photos of:

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Domicile Certificate / Ration Card / Voter ID
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank account details (including IFSC)

6. Complete Declaration

Tick all required checkboxes to accept the self-declaration statements.

7. Final Submission

  1. Review all entries carefully.
  2. Click Submit.
  3. Note down or screenshot the confirmation message indicating successful e-KYC.
Required Documents Checklist:
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Samagra (Family) ID
  • Aadhaar-linked mobile number
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Domicile / Ration Card / Voter ID
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank account details (with IFSC)

Important Notes

  • Use only the official portal for e-KYC.
  • The process is free—avoid third-party charges.
  • Complete your e-KYC before the deadline to continue receiving benefits.
